# IDsvault Legal & Transactional Framework

This document outlines the operational and escrow/payment mechanics of the IDsvault platform, establishing a safe, compliant digital identity brokerage model.

## Independent Brokerage Desk Identity
IDsvault is an independent, premium digital-identity brokerage desk. It is NOT a registered financial institution, licensed bank, or registered escrow agent under banking laws. It provides human broker-supervised custody and step-by-step transaction workflow coordination. It maintains no official affiliation, license, or endorsement with host platforms (including Instagram/Meta, X Corp, or Telegram).

## Escrow & Payment Stack
For all deal sizes, funds are held in IDsvault's designated broker account — strictly segregated from operating funds — until the buyer confirms full ownership on the live transfer call.

### Authorized Payment Channels
- **Primary:** IDsvault designated broker account (UPI/bank)
- **International:** Wise, SWIFT, crypto (USDC/USDT)
- **Third-party escrow (optional for very high-value deals, client request only):** Escrow.com

## Legal Safety & Risk Protection Policy
1. **Platform Risk Disclosures (IG & X):** Trading handles violates host platform Terms of Service (ToS). Host platforms reserve immediate, absolute right to reclaim, quarantine, or terminate handles. IDsvault operates as a transaction facilitator and offers zero warranties or survival guarantees post-transfer.
2. **KYC Verification Thresholds:** Identity Verification (KYC) is strictly required for all transactions above $1,000 USD equivalent, regardless of the chosen payment method.
3. **Refund Commitment:** In the event that a coordinate transfer fails or is determined permanently compromised, the held payments are refunded in full to the buyer within 3 business hours.
4. **Governing Law:** This framework and all brokerage transactions are governed by the laws of India, with exclusive dispute resolution and arbitration based in Hyderabad, India.
